About the Author

An author who knows how to spark curiosity and bring big ideas to life for young readers.

Yvonne Kilpatrick, the author of this delightful tale, has a gift for seeing the world through a child’s eyes. Her storytelling weaves together creativity, simplicity, and depth, inviting readers to not only enjoy the story but reflect on its meaning. By blending vivid imagery with relatable scenarios, Yvonne creates a world where children and adults alike can connect, wonder, and dream.

About the Book

What If Leaves Were Heavy?

Imagine a world where the simplest things, like leaves, are not as they seem. This enchanting book dares to ask that very question and more. With each turn of the page, readers are drawn into a playful yet meaningful journey that explores how one small change could impact everything. From the challenges of everyday life to the brilliance of God’s design, “What If Leaves Were Heavy?” is a heartfelt reminder of the beauty in the little details of life.
